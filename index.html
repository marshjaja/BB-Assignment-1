IMAGE 1

Vintage Oil Painting Landscape

In this piece, I aimed to created a vintage, aged aesthetic reminiscent of old paintings.

  • Noise effect: Added to replicate the grainy texture often seen in older oil paintings, enhancing the sense that the piece is worn.
  • Crack canvas overlay: Applied a cracked canvas pattern to simulate the appearance of an aged, deteriorating painting. This visual texture suggests a sense of nostalgia and history.
  • Level adjustments: Enhanced the contrast, giving it a stronger, more dramatic presence.
  • Vibrance enhancement: Boosted vibrance to intensify the greens and earth tones.
  • Lens filter: A warm filter was applied to create a soft, nostalgic atmosphere, evoking feelings of memory and time passing.

IMAGE 2

Dubai Starry Sky Double Exposure

  • Image blending: Combined three images to create one cohesive composition.
  • Overlay technique: Layered two images and used the brush tool to reveal parts of each, blending the cityscape with the starry sky.
  • Gradient effect: Applied a light gradient transition to smoothly blend the city lights into the cosmic background, enhancing depth and atmosphere.

Surreal Got Milk? Advertisement

  • Typography focus: Chose Avenir Condensed Next Regular and adjusted tracking and kerning to evoke the playful style of the original Got Milk? branding.
  • HSL adjustment: Adjusted the hue, saturation, and lightness to change the colour of some macarons for visual variety.
